T-BREW

Craft Beer

Welcome to the world of craft brewing, where passion and tradition meet! In Halstenbek, known as the cradle of the forest, I warmly welcome you to my home brewery, where every beer is brewed with love and dedication.

As a true lover of hops, I understand that the secret to great beer lies in the quality of ingredients. That's why I only use the finest hop varieties to brew my unique beers. Every sip tells the story of my passion for craftsmanship and flavor.

The brewery is built up on great Grainfather equipment. It has a volume of 20l per batch.

T-Brew Homebrewing is a litte project for trying things that you cannot buy in a supermarket. T stands for tea. So be excited what's coming with the next brew.

TEA means Camellia Sinensis and Tradition English Ale.
